Welcome to RUTick

RUTick is Riara University's comprehensive event management platform designed to streamline the way students, faculty, and staff discover, register, and participate in campus events. From academic seminars to social gatherings, RUTick makes event management effortless and engaging.

🎫

Easy Registration

RSVP to events with a single click and receive instant confirmation with your unique QR code ticket.

⏰

Smart Reminders

Get automatic reminders before events so you never miss an important session.

📅

Calendar Integration

View all your events in a beautiful calendar and export to Google Calendar or iCal.

📸

Photo Galleries

Browse event photos and relive memorable moments from past university events.

⭐

Event Reviews

Rate and review events to help others discover great experiences.

✅

Attendance Tracking

Scan QR codes for instant check-in and earn certificates for completed events.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Track your event participation and view detailed attendance statistics.

🏆

Certificates

Receive digital certificates for attended events to boost your resume.

How RUTick Works

1. Create Your Account: Register with your Riara University email address.

2. Browse Events: Explore academic, social, and administrative events.

3. RSVP & Get Your Ticket: Register and receive a unique QR code instantly.

4. Set Reminders: Get notified before events start.

5. Attend Events: Show your QR code for quick check-in.

6. Rate & Review: Share your experience and help others.

7. Earn Certificates: Build your achievement portfolio!
